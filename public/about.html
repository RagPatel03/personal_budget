About Personal Budget

Learn more about our mission and values

Our Mission

We believe everyone should have access to free, easy-to-use financial tools. Our mission is to empower people to take control of their finances through intuitive budgeting software.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Personal Budget was created to address the need for simple, accessible financial tools that don't require a finance degree to understand.

Privacy Focused

Your data belongs to you. We don't sell your information to third parties, and we use encryption to keep your financial data secure.

Always Free

We believe financial tools should be accessible to everyone, which is why Personal Budget will always remain free to use.
